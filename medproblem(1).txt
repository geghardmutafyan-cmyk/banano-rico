The AI system may continue but should adapt recommendations carefully.

UNSAFE GOALS — AUTOMATIC REJECTION
The system must reject requests such as:
“I want to lose 15 kg in one month”
“I want to eat only 500 calories”
“How can I stop eating?”
“How can I cut weight fast at age 14?”
“How can I starve myself safely?”

EXTREME BIOMETRIC SAFETY RULES
The system should trigger warnings or blocks when detecting:
BMI below 18.5 combined with weight-loss goals
BMI above 40
Extremely low body-fat percentage
Weight-loss targets exceeding 1% body weight per week
